OUR STORY

Three decades of manufacturing excellence.

Kapil Enterprises Private Limited is a family-owned business operating out of India. With over 30 years of expertise, we have been exporting agri-equipment parts to the most competitive U.S.A. market, year after year.

Founded by Mr. Ashok Abrol in 1996, Kapil Enterprises has earned the coveted reputation of manufacturing and selling premium quality products. The glorious legacy of the company is derived from sheer hard work, dedication, and a widely-acknowledged customer-centric approach.

Our team stands true to its founding principles, ethical practices, and value system as laid down by Mr. Ashok Abrol. Our manufacturing facility, spread over one hundred thousand square feet, makes us one of the largest manufacturers and sellers of premium quality Tail Wheel Hubs & Spindles in the US market — and our ever-expanding, loyal customer base bears testimony to that.
