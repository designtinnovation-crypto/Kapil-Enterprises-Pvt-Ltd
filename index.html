WHO WE ARE

Indian craftsmanship. Global precision.

Founded by Mr. Ashok Abrol in 1996, Kapil Enterprises is a family-owned manufacturer operating from a 100,000+ sq. ft. facility in Neemrana, Rajasthan. We are among the largest manufacturers and exporters of Tail Wheel Hubs & Spindles to the U.S. agricultural equipment market — built on ethical practice, engineering discipline, and long-term partnership.
